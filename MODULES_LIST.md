# School Management System - Modules List

This document outlines all the panels and their respective modules available in the School Management System.

## 1. Authentication & Onboarding
- School Registration
- Teacher Registration
- Parent Registration
- Login Page
- Forgot Password
- Pending Approval Screen

## 2. Super Admin Panel
- **Overview / Dashboard**: High-level metrics across all tenants.
- **Tenants List**: View all registered schools.
- **Tenant Management**: Approve, reject, or manage tenant statuses.
- **Tenant Details**: Deep dive into specific tenant configurations.
- **Subscriptions List**: Track active subscriptions across tenants.
- **Plan Management**: Create, edit, and delete pricing tiers and limits.
- **Support Tickets**: Manage support requests from tenants.
- **Audit Logs**: Track global actions and changes.
- **Notification Broadcast**: Send global alerts to all tenants.
- **Feature Flags**: Toggle beta features globally.
- **Role Permissions**: Manage super admin roles.
- **Branding Settings**: Global white-labeling configurations.
- **License Usage**: Monitor global resource consumption.

## 3. Admin Panel (School Management)
- **Admin Overview**: School-specific metrics and shortcuts.
- **Billing Dashboard & Upgrade Plan**: Manage school subscription and payments.
- **Class Management**: Create and manage classes/sections.
- **Timetable Management**: Schedule classes and assign teachers.
- **Student Management**: Add, edit, and view student records.
- **Staff Assignment**: Manage teacher subject assignments.
- **Attendance Management**: Track and view school-wide attendance.
- **Exam Management**: Schedule exams and manage grading rubrics.
- **Fee Management**: Track fee payments, dues, and generate receipts.
- **Noticeboard**: Publish announcements to teachers, students, or parents.
- **Transport Management**: Manage vehicles, routes, and GPS tracking.
- **Hostel Management**: Manage rooms, allocations, and capacities.
- **Library Management**: Manage book inventory and issuing.
- **Inventory Management**: Track school assets and supplies.
- **Health Records**: Maintain student medical data.
- **HR & Payroll Management**: Manage staff salaries and details.
- **Complaint Redressal**: Handle issues raised by parents/students.
- **Alumni Management**: Maintain records of graduated students.
- **Document Management**: Store and organize school documents.
- **Multi-Branch Management**: Switch between different school branches.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Generate comprehensive academic/financial reports.
- **Report Template Builder**: Customize report cards.
- **API Integrations**: Manage third-party keys (e.g., payment gateways).
- **Environment Setup**: System configuration.
- **Link Generator**: Generate invite links for staff/parents.

## 4. Teacher Panel
- **Teacher Dashboard**: Quick overview of today's classes and tasks.
- **Class Roster**: View assigned students.
- **Teacher Timetable**: Personal schedule view.
- **Attendance**: Mark daily class attendance.
- **Grades**: Input exam marks and assessments.
- **Homework Management**: Assign and track homework.
- **Lesson Plans**: Plan syllabus and daily lessons.
- **Teacher Noticeboard**: View admin announcements.
- **Calendar**: Academic calendar view.
- **PTM Scheduler**: Manage Parent-Teacher meeting requests.
- **Teacher Leave**: Apply for and track leave requests.
- **Performance Tracking**: Analyze student progress.
- **Resource Sharing**: Upload study materials.
- **Chat**: Communicate with parents and students.

## 5. Parent Panel
- **Parent Dashboard**: Overview of their child's academic status.
- **Student Overview**: Detailed view of grades, attendance, and fees.
- **Homework Overview**: Track assigned and pending homework.
- **Calendar**: View school holidays and events.
- **Canteen**: View menus or manage meal plans.
- **Parent Noticeboard**: View school and teacher announcements.
- **Chat**: Communicate with assigned class teachers.
